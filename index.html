About Me

As a past career educator who had taught a myriad of grade levels for 13 years, I see myself as a lifetime learner who loves to read and grow as a person. At Shiprock High School I taught professional development meetings once a week with another Reading Specialist, as well as went into content area classrooms and taught Reading in others’ classrooms. I served as a resource for teachers and a researcher and writer for the school in general. I’m good with communicating with others in all forms including technologically, designating duties, and guiding others to implement needed practices. Reading, technology, and traveling are my main passions in life. I love learning and am devoted to all aspects of education so I am willing to take additional classes or attend workshops as needed. Please email or give me a call to set up an appointment time to discuss services that I can provide.
